Pronoun
Word used in place of a noun. The six types of pronouns are: 
(1) personal (I, we, you, he, she, it, they); (2) interrogative 
(who, which, what); (3) relative (who, which, that); (4) 
demonstrative (this, that, these, those); (5) indefinite (one, 
nobody, someone, anything, etc.) (6) reflexive (myself, 
yourselves, himself, etc.).
