You need not memorize the meaning of sentence, subject, verb, and the 
like. But read the definitions in this special glossary, whenever you have a 
chance. After a few readings you will know the meaning of most of these 
thirty-five key terms. Then, if you begin to misuse one of the parts of 
speech, a phrase, or clause, your mind will become more alert and you'll 
correct the error before it occurs. The terms selected for this glossary are 
those you'll need most often in technical writing. The definitions are short 
and easy to understand and use. Study this glossary and see how useful 
grammar can be.
